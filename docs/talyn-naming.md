# Why Talyn?

The biblical creature "[Leviathan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leviathan)" never really clicked with me. The only Leviathan I truly knew and loved was **[Moya](https://farscape.fandom.com/wiki/Moya)** from the sci-fi series *[Farscape](https://farscape.fandom.com/wiki/Farscape)*.

I watched *Farscape* from the pilot episode all the way to *[The Peacekeeper Wars](https://farscape.fandom.com/wiki/Farscape:_The_Peacekeeper_Wars)*. When I started pouring a swarm of AI coding agents into this project — watching them rapidly implement features, fix bugs, and shape the codebase — it strongly reminded me of Moya’s little **DRDs** ([Diagnostic Repair Drones](https://farscape.fandom.com/wiki/Diagnostic_Repair_Drone)) busily working inside her.

In that moment, it felt perfect: the original Leviathan (Moya) was “giving birth” to her powerful hybrid offspring — **[Talyn](https://farscape.fandom.com/wiki/Talyn)**.

So I renamed the project **Talyn** — a Leviathan-hybrid, born from the original vision, strengthened and evolved with the help of its DRD swarm.
